Pacific Rhododendron

Alternative Names

  • California Rosebay
  • Coast Rhododendron
  • Big Leaf Rhododendron
  • California Rhododendron

Size

10-25 feet tall and 5-10 feet wide

Geography

The Pacific Rhododendron is a native, broadleaf evergreen shrub found along the Pacific Coast of North America, ranging from British Columbia to Monterey County, California. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils, typically inhabiting low- to middle-elevation coniferous forest understories, slopes, and ridges.

Climate

The Pacific rhododendron thrives in a temperate, maritime climate, characterized by cool, wet winters and mild, relatively dry summers, typically found from southern British Columbia to Northern California.
